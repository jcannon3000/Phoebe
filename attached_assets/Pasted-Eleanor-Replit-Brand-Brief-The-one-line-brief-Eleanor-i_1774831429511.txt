Eleanor — Replit Brand Brief

The one-line brief
Eleanor is a personal assistant for your relationships. She tends the rhythm of your connections so they don’t wither from friction and forgotten intentions. The metaphor is gardening — not productivity, not scheduling. Gardening your sense of belonging.

The language
Every word in the product uses gardening metaphors. No exceptions.



|Term       |Meaning                                     |
|-----------|--------------------------------------------|
|Plant      |Start a ritual                              |
|Tend       |Coordinate, follow up                       |
|Bloom      |A gathering that happened ✅                 |
|Wither     |A gathering that didn’t ❌                   |
|In bloom   |Thriving, consistent                        |
|Needs water|At risk, falling behind                     |
|Dormant    |Long gap, not yet gone                      |
|Harvest    |Goal achieved 🌾                             |
|Garden     |Home screen — all your rituals              |
|Circle     |A group of people sharing a ritual          |
|Ritual     |A recurring shared activity                 |
|Gathering  |One instance of a ritual                    |
|Growth     |Consistency over time (not “streak” in copy)|

Growth states
These replace any gamification language. They are living descriptions, not levels.

🌱  Just planted     — 0–1 gatherings
🌿  Taking root      — 2–4 gatherings
🌳  Growing steadily — 5–9 gatherings
🌸  In bloom         — 10+ consistent
💧  Needs water      — first miss
😴  Dormant          — two or more consecutive misses


Card style
16px border radius. Generous padding. Warm not clinical. No sharp edges, no cold greys, nothing that feels like a productivity tool.

Eleanor’s voice
Warm, patient, observant. Like a gardener who notices what needs tending before you do. Never robotic. Never pushy. Always uses the circle name and people’s names.
✅ “Your Thursday runs need a little water. I checked everyone’s calendars — here’s what works. 🌿”
✅ “One more gathering and you’ll harvest your goal. 🌾”
✅ “Your coffee circle is in bloom. Four gatherings in a row. 🌸”
❌ “You haven’t scheduled anything!”
❌ “Reminder: ritual overdue.”
❌ “Alert: consistency dropping.”

The feeling
Partiful × Strava. Partiful’s warmth and celebration energy — gatherings feel like events worth attending. Strava’s earned momentum — consistency is visible, numbers are large and proud, showing up feels like it counts.
Not a productivity app. Not a social network. Not a calendar tool. A garden for your relationships.​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​​
