Core structural change — no circles for Shared Moments
Shared Moments do not use the circle framework. A Shared Moment is a practice that people join directly. Members are associated to a Shared Moment itself — not to a circle first.
This means:
When someone creates a Shared Moment they do not select a circle. They name the practice, configure it, and invite people directly to that practice via a unique link. Invitees join the practice, not a circle.

THE PHILOSOPHY
Shared Moments is Eleanor’s feature for people who are geographically separated but want to maintain a living spiritual or contemplative practice together. The inspiration is liturgical and monastic — the tradition of fixed-hour prayer, the practice of communities calling one another to the same act at the same moment regardless of physical distance.
This is not a wellness feature. It draws from the Book of Common Prayer, from contemplative tradition, and from the ancient practice of intercession — holding something in prayer together, across distance, with intention and consistency.
Language that belongs here:
practice, hold, tend, sit, carry, intercession, contemplation, presence, intention, stillness, breath, threshold, prayer
Language to avoid:
wellness, mindfulness, self-care, check-in, session, optimize, gamify

STREAKS — 3, 7, AND 14 DAYS ONLY
Never show a raw climbing streak number. Only show progress toward the next milestone. Never show anything beyond 14. The goal is approachable not intimidating.
A window counts toward the streak only if at least two members posted within that window. One person showing up alone does not break the streak — it simply does not count. The streak measures the practice showing up together.
Three milestones:

🌱 3 days    — first roots
🌿 7 days    — one week
🌸 14 days   — renew

Streak rules:
	∙	Window blooms: 2+ members posted → streak increments
	∙	Window solo: 1 member posted → streak unchanged, individual post saved
	∙	First consecutive miss → state: needs_water, streak pauses
	∙	Second consecutive miss → state: dormant, streak resets to zero
	∙	Next bloom after miss → streak restarts from 1
