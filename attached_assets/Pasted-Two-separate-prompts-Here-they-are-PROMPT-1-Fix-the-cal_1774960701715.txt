Rules for all invite emails:
Remove entirely:
	∙	“WHAT IS ELEANOR?” section
	∙	“THIS PRACTICE” section with the tabular When/Window/Goal/How/Prompt breakdown
	∙	“HOW IT WORKS” section
	∙	Any explanation of how Eleanor works
	∙	Clock times (replace with time of day language — morning, afternoon, evening)
	∙	The word “window”
	∙	“1 hour to log each time”
The personal link must appear high in the email — within the first four lines. Never buried at the bottom.
All of this information — what Eleanor is, how the practice works, the full prayer text — is available when they tap their personal link. The email’s only job is to get them to tap the link.
